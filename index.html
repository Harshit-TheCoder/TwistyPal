Step 1: Make the White Cross

Solve the white edges to form a plus sign (cross) on the white face. Make sure the side colors of the edges also match the center colors.

Step 2A: Insert White Corners

Use the right-hand algorithm R' D' R D to insert white corners into the bottom layer one by one.

Step 2B: Insert White Corners (Left Side)

If the white corner is on the left side, use L D L' D' to insert it into place.

Step 3A: Insert Edge (Right)
